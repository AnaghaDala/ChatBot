- What are the must-visit places in Mumbai?
- Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and Elephanta Caves are among the must-visit places in Mumbai.

- List some famous tourist spots in Agra.
- The Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and Fatehpur Sikri are iconic tourist spots in Agra.

- Tell me about the attractions in Varanasi.
- Varanasi is famous for the Ghats along the Ganges, Kashi Vishwanath Temple, and Sarnath.

- Which places should one explore in Udaipur?
- City Palace, Lake Pichola, and Jag Mandir are noteworthy places to explore in Udaipur.

- What are the popular landmarks in Kolkata?
- Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge, and Dakshineswar Kali Temple are iconic landmarks in Kolkata.

- Name some historical sites in Hampi.
- Virupaksha Temple, Hampi Bazaar, and Lotus Mahal are significant historical sites in Hampi.

- What are the main attractions in Rishikesh?
- The Ganges River, Laxman Jhula, and The Beatles Ashram are main attractions in Rishikesh.

- Tell me about the famous places in Goa.
- Baga Beach, Basilica of Bom Jesus, and Dudhsagar Waterfalls are famous places to visit in Goa.

- Which places are popular for trekking in Himachal Pradesh?
- Rohtang Pass, Triund, and Kheerganga are popular trekking destinations in Himachal Pradesh.

- What are the famous places in Mangalore, Karnataka?
- Mangaladevi Temple, Panambur Beach, and St. Aloysius Chapel are some of the famous places in Mangalore.

- Name the key attractions in Udupi, Karnataka.
- Sri Krishna Temple, Malpe Beach, and Anantheshwara Temple are notable attractions in Udupi.

- What are the must-visit places in Mysore, Karnataka?
- Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hills, and Brindavan Gardens are must-visit places in Mysore.

- Mention some prominent places in Bangalore, Karnataka.
- Lalbagh Botanical Garden, Bangalore Palace, and Cubbon Park are prominent places in Bangalore.

- What are the top attractions in Chennai, Tamil Nadu?
- Marina Beach, Kapaleeshwarar Temple, and Fort St. George are top attractions in Chennai.

- List some famous places in Kerala.
- Alleppey Backwaters, Munnar Tea Gardens, and Periyar National Park are famous places in Kerala.
